Webcam Image Classification using a pre-trained customized model and p5.js

Money Classifier 🇵🇱🤝🇺🇸

This is a demonstration of image classification using a model trained with Google's Teachable Machine project. The model can determine whether a bill is Polish Złoty, United States Dollar, Ukrainian Hryvnia, or neither. Try it our by getting Polish Złoty, US Dollars, or Ukrainian Hryvnia and placing it about 1.0 foot from the camera clearly. Enjoy!
